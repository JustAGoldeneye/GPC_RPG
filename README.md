## Controls

### Gameplay Controls

* W - Walk forward
* A - Turn left
* D - Turn right
* Q - Turn camera left
* E - Turn camera right
* R - Reset camera position
* SPACE - Jump
* RIGHT CONTROL - Normal attack

### Menu Controls (Including Battle Menu)

* Arrow keys - Navigate on UI
* Enter - Confirm
* Backspace - Cancel
